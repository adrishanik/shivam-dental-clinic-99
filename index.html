Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Abhinandan Patra BDS, MDS

Dr. Abhinandan Patra leads Shivam Dental Clinic in Tarakeswar, providing focused treatment across orthodontics (braces), root canal therapy, dento-facial trauma and children's dentistry. The clinic is built around unhurried, one-on-one consultations so every treatment plan is explained clearly before it begins.

What We Treat

Dental care for every stage of the smile

Braces & Orthodontics

Corrective treatment for misaligned teeth and bite issues, for both children and adults.

Root Canal Treatment

Precise, comfortable root canal therapy to relieve pain and save the natural tooth.

Paediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care that builds healthy habits early on.

Dento-Facial Trauma

Emergency care for chipped, cracked or injured teeth and facial dental trauma.

Also offering general check-ups, cleaning and preventive care — ask us about a full treatment list during your visit.
